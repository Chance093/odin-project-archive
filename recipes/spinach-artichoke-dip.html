Slow Cooker Spinach-Artichoke Dip

This slow cooker spinach-artichoke dip is easy to make and delicious!

This is how I make my homemade Spinach Artichoke Dip! I love to bring this recipe to potlucks. Always gets a ton of compliments. You will not regret making this!

Ingredients

  • 1 (14 ounce) can artichoke hearts, drained and chopped
  • 1 (9 ounce) package frozen spinach
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese
  • ¾ cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ cup shredded Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ cup milk
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine artichokes, spinach, cream cheese, mozzarella cheese, Parmesan cheese, milk, salt, and pepper in the bottom of a slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on High, stirring occasionally to combine, for 2 hours.
